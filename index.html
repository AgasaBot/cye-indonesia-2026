What is CYE

What is CYE?

The CYE Competition — short for Creative Young Entrepreneur — is a pitch competition dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurial spirit among today's brightest minds. It's a platform where aspiring founders showcase their groundbreaking businesses, pitch them live, and compete for a chance to turn their dreams into reality.

Our vision

VISION

Our vision is simple yet profound: to empower the next generation of entrepreneurs to transform their businesses and drive positive change in the world. We believe entrepreneurship is the driving force behind innovation, economic growth, and social progress. Through the CYE Competition, we aim to identify, nurture, and support the most promising entrepreneurs on their journey to success.

Who can join

How it works

From application to the Taiwan stage in six steps.

It starts with an online submission and narrows down to the live finals in Jakarta. Pitching is in English.

1

Apply & submit

From July, submit your concept form, headshot and a 2-minute pitch video. Deadline 31 Aug 2026.

2

Jury review

The jury reviews every entry and shortlists the Top 30 strongest businesses.

3

Mentorship for Top 20

Through September, the Top 30 are mentored by past CYE winners, reviewed, and shortlisted down to the Top 20 semi-finalists.Sep 2026

4

Preliminary round in Jakarta

At the National Final, the Top 20 pitch live. The judges narrow the field to the Top 4.3 Oct 2026

5

Final round

The Top 4 pitch again with judge Q&A — and one national winner is crowned.3 Oct 2026

★

Win & advance

The winner represents Indonesia at the 2027 JCI ASPAC in Taiwan.
